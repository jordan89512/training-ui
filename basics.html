The Basics:
Module 1

Clocking In:   It is important that you keep track of your time while at work. Most important is when you clock in or out of your shift for the day. If you are scheduled to work from 6:00am - 2:30pm for example, make sure you are clocked in at least 5 minutes prior or no later than 6:00am exactly. If you are cleared to work overtime by your supervisor, it's okay to show up earlier, just make sure to ask beforehand unless otherwise instructed. The same applies to staying later.

Clocking into jobs: To clock into a job, you will need the traveler for the relevant job you are working on. Go to the timeclock and enter your employee id, select 'start wo' and enter requested information, job number, operation number etc. Once the relevant information is entered, hit the F2 button to complete your clock in and begin the job.

Report to your supervisor:   The Supervisor of Dept. 10 is Benny. He is who you will report to in order to be assigned your duties or to be trained accordingly. Pay close attention to what others are doing around you, where to get materials and how to perform the job duties correctly for what task you are assigned. You will be expected to know when to do what without being told once you are fully trained.

If supervisor is absent or preoccupied:   Feel free to ask someone. There are plenty of helpful people to guide you if you don't know where to start. The easiest thing to do would be to ask someone if they need help with what they are working on.
